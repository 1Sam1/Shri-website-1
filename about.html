About SHRI

Committed to providing quality manpower across Mumbai and beyond.

Our Story

SHRI was founded with a mission to bridge the gap between businesses and reliable, skilled manpower. We operate in Mumbai with a focus on hospitality, catering, events, and related industries.

Over the years, we have supplied trained professionals for hotels, wedding caterers, event planners, and more. Our team is selected carefully, trained thoroughly, and managed diligently to ensure your operations run smoothly.

Why Choose SHRI?

  • Highly trained and trustworthy staff
  • Flexible contracts—temporary or permanent
  • On-site supervision and performance tracking
  • Quick deployment and responsive support
